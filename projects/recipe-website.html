My Dad's Quorn Chilli

um so yeah this is the recipe and stuff, make sure you have a big pan (◕‿◕✿)

Ingredients

  • Cumin Seeds
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Chili Pepper
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 250g Quorn Mince
  • 1 Tablespoon of Tomato Paste
  • 2 Glasses of Red Wine
  • 1 Tin of Kidney Beans in Chili Sauce
  • 1 Vegetable Stock Cube
  • Pint of Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chili Powder
  • Coriander

Instructions

  1. Fry (medium low heat) cumin seeds in oil until start to darken (30 secs). Add onion and chilli and fry till softened (3-5 mins). Add 2 cloves garlic chopped finely
  2. Add quorn mince (250-300g). Stir well
  3. Add tablespoon tomato paste and a glass of red wine. Coat well and fry for a few mins till wine has reduced
  4. Add tin of beans in chilli sauce, stir well and fry for a couple of mins
  5. Boil kettle and dissolve a veg stock cube in a pint of boiling water. Add to pan. Bring to boil then reduce heat and simmer for 10 mins until water has reduced and looks like chilli
  6. Season with salt, pepper, half teaspoon chilli powder and a teaspoon of coriander powder. Enjoy :3
